Who We Are

Clean, accessible energy that’s easy to choose, and easy to feel good about.

Diamond Energy is a leading enabler of clean, accessible electricity, only contracting with a growing portfolio of renewable energy generators across Australia. We enable households and businesses to play an active role in the future of energy transition by integrating their solar and batteries through our Virtual Power Plant. We make it easy to choose better — on service, on values, and on impact.
